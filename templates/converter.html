About File Converter

This tool converts files directly in your browser using VERT (a privacy-focused, open-source file converter). All conversions happen locally - your files never leave your device.

Supported formats: Images (PNG, JPG, WEBP, SVG, etc.), Audio (MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, etc.), Documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, etc.), and Video (MP4, AVI, MKV, etc.).

Quick Tips

  • Drag and drop files directly into the converter area
  • Select your desired output format from the dropdown
  • Click "Convert" and download your converted file
  • All processing happens in your browser - files stay private
  • Large video conversions may take several minutes
